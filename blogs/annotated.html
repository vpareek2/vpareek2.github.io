The Annotated Series

The Annotated Series is a set of blog posts containing annotated implementations of popular Machine Learning algorithms and architectures. This is inspired by "The Annotated Transformer" written by Professor Rush when he was at Harvard. There is a possibility that I made errors in the implementations or explanations, if you find one feel free to let me know. I use this series as a learning exercise to understand model architectures better.
